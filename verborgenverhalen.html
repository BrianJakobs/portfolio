About the project

How I can help you

The months before this project I was a Visual Design intern at Driebit. I was also in my final year looking for a graduation assignment. Driebit was at this time working at the current redesigned website of the Holland Open Air Museum and where hoping to do the online collection next. This situation became a huge opportunity to start creating an entire new expierence for users to experience the online collection. So I set out to do exactly this.

The Holland Open Air Museum Arnhem has an extensive digtial collection of objects related to the museum. The current website to show these objects however is not very user friendly or appealing to use.
